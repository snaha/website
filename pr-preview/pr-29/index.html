We’re a small and independent team defending privacy, freedom and more generally, human interests in software products.

We build for the web — websites, web applications, web3 dapps & other crypto-related projects.
Attila and David met back in 2012, while working together at Prezi. In 2019, they co-founded the Felfele Foundation. Shortly after that, they met with Vojtech while working with the Swarm Foundation. Since them Attila, David and Vojtech have stuck together. They’ve worked with different teams at Logos and co-founded Snaha together. Besides helping selected clients, Snaha is bootstrapping Kalkul and developing Diète, its own open-source design system.
